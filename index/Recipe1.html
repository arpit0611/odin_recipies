Oatmeal Cookie Apple Crisp

Description :

This is by far the best apple dessert I've ever tasted! The topping tastes just

like an oatmeal cookie. It's the perfect combination of crunchy, chewy cookie and

buttery soft apples. Great dessert for a party or family gathering (double the

recipe in a 9x13-inch baking dish) or to make just for yourself on a chilly evening. Simply delicious

Ingredients

Filling :

  • 6 cups peeled and sliced apples
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour

Topping :

  • ¾ cup old-fashioned rolled oats
  • ¾ cup all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup brown sugar

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Step 2

    3. Mix apples, white sugar, 1 tablespoon flour, cinnamon, and salt together in a bowl; spread into an 8-inch square baking dish.

  3. Step 3

    4. Combine oats, 3/4 cup flour, brown sugar, baking soda, and baking powder together in a separate bowl; add butter and mix until crumbly. Crumble topping evenly over apple filling using your hands.

  4. Step 4

    5. Bake in the preheated oven until topping is lightly browned, 30 to 40 minutes.
